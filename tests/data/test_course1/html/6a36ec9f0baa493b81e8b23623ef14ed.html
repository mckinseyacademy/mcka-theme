The members of your group appear below. Click Email to send a message to an individual. Click Email your entire team to send a message to the full group. You may also use the button at the bottom of the Project Navigator () to participate in a private discussion with your group members.

We have randomly assigned a group coordinator for each team via email. If you are unsure of who is the coordinator for your group, please check your welcome email to the course, “Team Management: Week 1.”

Group coordinators perform two critical functions:

  1. Organize the group’s meetings, including the group kickoff
  2. Upload the group’s submissions to the course platform following working sessions
